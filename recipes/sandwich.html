Sandwich

Description

Two pieces of bread with literally anything between them.

Ingredients

  • Two slices of bread
  • Anything that will fit between the bread
  • Mayonnaise, mustard, or another condiment (optional)

Process

  1. Spread condiments on one side of each slice of bread
  2. Place filling atop the condiments of one slice
  3. Place remaining slice, condiment side down, atop the filling
  4. Cut in half if desired, and serve
